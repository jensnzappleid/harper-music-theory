⏱ What is a Time Signature?
A time signature is two numbers at the start of music — like a fraction!
Top = how many beats per bar 🔢
Bottom = what note gets ONE beat
4
4

💡 Bottom number guide:
2 = Minim (𝅗𝅥) gets 1 beat
4 = Crotchet (♩) gets 1 beat
8 = Quaver (♪) gets 1 beat
